Healthy Carrot Pancakes

Imagine to start your day with a sweet breakfast and yet nutritious....hard to believe??

Well...with this recipe I will show that it is posible! You will think that you are eating a mini carrot cake as a breakfast

And I'm sure these pancakes will become your favorites, so I won't miss more time talking / writing and let's cook!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 measure powdered stevia
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrot
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk or water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • coconut or canola oil for cooking

Directions:

  1. Put all ingredients (beside carrots) to a blender and blend on high for 30 seconds or until completely smooth.
  2. Add the carrots, stir (DO NOT BLEND) everything with a spoon and set batter aside to rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Heat a light coat of coconut or canola oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Stir the batter again with the spoon and drop 2 tablespoons of the batter for each pancake into the skillet.
    Depending of the size of your skillet you can put 3 or 4 pancakes at the same time.
  5. Cook until panckes slightly puff up and you see a few bubbles along the edges, 2-4 minutes.
  6. Flip pancakes until golden brown on underside. If you find that pancakes are browning too quickly,
    then you need to lower the heat.
  7. Once they are cooked, put pancakes in a plate with a cloth or absorbent paper.
  8. Repeat with more oil and remaining batter. Makes 5 to 6 pancakes total.
  9. Now its time to serve them with your favorites toppings like blueberrys, greek yogurt or sugar free syrop!
